About

At the time of coding, container-queries are supported in Chrome Cannery under the chrome://flags -> "Enable CSS Container Queries".

Keep in mind that this demo uses the syntax and features the version of Chrome Cannery supported on 27th June 2021. Container-queries are still in the process of standardization and subject to changes, thus this demo might stop working.

Best resource for following the CQ development is ccswg project board on Github.
